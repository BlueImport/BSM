Why Data Science

Data Science is all about turning noise into meaning. Finding trends in data is nothing new, to be sure. What we do with that data is what really matters.

Many of the concepts we use to explore the world around us have long been established. The real joy comes from finding useful application for those concepts in modern day problems. Expanding old ideas with new technology is how we fuel the engines of progress.
