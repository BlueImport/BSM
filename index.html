Why Data Science

Data Science is the art of taking in noise, and deciphering a meaning. Finding trends in data is nothing new, to be sure. What we do with that data is what really matters.

It is so exciting to take a very complicated concept and using it to answer a present day question. Expanding old ideas with new technology is how we throttle the engines of progress.

What I Think

My mission is to ultimately bring Data Science, and the logic used therein, to bear against some of the toughest problems facing humanity today. The field has contributed much to corporations seeking the best ad marketing campaign, or political forecasting. I believe we can use these same skills to unlock a more fulfilling future.

Future Goals

It's important to consider that any machine learning algorithm is only as good as the intuition provided in it's application. I'd like to see myself contributing to a progressive and diverse future society. Either through tracking new planets to maybe call home, or helping a new driver find that perfect first car. Data is all around us, and it's up to us to mold it into a force for a positive future.
